Chicken curry

Description

This chicken curry is a classic Indian dish with tender chicken cooked in a rich onion-tomato gravy infused with aromatic spices. Perfect with rice or roti for a hearty lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chilli powder, and garam masala. Stir, then add chicken pieces.
  3. Mix well, add chopped tomatoes, and cook until chicken is tender and oil separates.
  4. Add water to adjust consistency. Simmer for 5 minutes, garnish with coriander leaves, and serve hot.
