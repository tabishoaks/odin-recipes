Chicken sandwich

Description

This chicken sandwich is a simple yet delicious meal, featuring juicy grilled chicken breast layered with fresh lettuce and ripe tomato slices, all tucked between perfectly toasted bread with a spread of creamy mayonnaise. Itâ€™s filling, easy to make, and ideal for a quick lunch, evening snack, or a light dinner when you want something tasty without spending too much time in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, and your choice of spices. Grill or pan-fry until fully cooked, then slice or shred it.
  2. Toast two slices of bread or a sandwich bun until golden brown.
  3. Spread mayonnaise or any preferred sauce on the bread. Place lettuce, tomato slices, and the cooked chicken on it.
  4. Top with cheese (optional) and the second slice of bread. Press lightly, cut in half, and serve warm.
